Welcome... and now, let's play some

Crystalline Blackjack!

New to the game? Hover your mouse here for the rules!

The way it works is simple!

At the start of each game the dealer picks a number and also assigns a new value to each suit.

Your goal is to click on the suits to add to your score. Match your score to exactly the same as the dealer's number to win!

If you go over the dealer's number, you bust! You can also choose the lucky button to add the value of one of the suits chosen at random, multiplied by 5. Careful, this is a quick way to end a game... for better or for worse!
